|       Mailing List       |                                [Zoho](https://www.zoho.com/)                                |
|  Hosting/Deployment/DNS  |                              [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com)                             |
|           Forms          |           [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com) and [Google](https://www.google.com)           |
